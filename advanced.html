Scenario 7: Photo Audit Relay

Role: Safety Lead / Supervisor Target: Native Camera API
Story: A small mechanical fluid leak is spotted. Your insurance carrier demands direct image verification attached to the liability file.
  1. Navigate over to the Hazard Reports view tab and tap "Report a Hazard".
  2. Set the report classification type dropdown strictly to Incident.
  3. Paste this exact string description: Slight hydraulic leak discovered on the rear axle of the lawn tractor.
  4. Tap into the dashed Photo Attachment target box area.
  5. Accept your device's native browser camera permission prompt. Snap a live photo or pull a dummy image file from your library.
  6. Tap "Submit Hazard Safety Report".
UX Observation Gate: Did the pipeline feel slow or timed out over mobile data? Note that the system routes images for cloud-processing—confirm it says photo was received instead of rendering a broken layout thumbnail.

Scenario 8: Safety Card Ledger

Role: Administrator Target: Data Tables & Filters
Story: Your crew operator completes their safety certification block. Log the parameters to legally clear them for commercial field duty.
  1. Tap the Admin primary layout tab, then open the Team Access sub-menu view.
  2. Tap into the amber sub-tab option titled "Skill & Training Certifications".
  3. Under the log certification menu, look at the worker dropdown. If empty, tap "Add Team Member" first and configure a fake user account using the testworker1@fakeco.com standard.
  4. Select your worker. Set type to OSHA 10-Hour Card or High-Heat Illness Prevention Course.
  5. Choose a date and tap "Log Certification Card". Test the search bar filter above the table.
UX Observation Gate: Did the data ledger append instantly? Did the certification status badge clearly pair text metadata with color labels (Active / Expiring) for low-vision accessibility?
